Welcome to my homepage. I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Thessaly. My research is primarily focused on finite fields and their applications.

I obtained my BSc in Mathematics from the Department of Mathematics of the University of Athens. Then I obtained my MSc in Pure Mathematics and my PhD in Mathematics from the Department of Mathematics and Applied Mathematics of the University of Crete.

After my studies, I held postdoc positions at the LabSEC of the Federal University of Santa Catarina and at Algebra and Number Theory Group of Sabanci University and I served as a Visiting Lecturer at the Department of Mathematics and Applied Mathematics of the University of Crete.

More details about my studies and my postdoctoral experience can be found at the cv section.

Last but not least, you may want to check at the Greek Algebra and Number Theory Seminar that I co-organize.
